My Story

With over 20 years of experience as a paramedic, I bring a deep commitment to service, precision, and critical thinking to everything I do. For the past five years, I’ve served as the Data Officer for my EMS agency, using data to improve patient care, streamline operations, and support strategic decision-making. Currently, I'm expanding my skill set through IBM’s Full Stack Developer and Data Engineer certificate programs, merging my field experience with a passion for technology. My goal is to build robust, user-friendly web applications and data systems that focus on streamline workflow and business continuity. I’m especially interested in leveraging data to drive innovation in public safety and health outcomes.
